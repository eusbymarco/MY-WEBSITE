About Me

Eusebious Makori is currently a student at the ALX foundation and passionate about software engineering, dedicated to building solutions that connect people, empower communities, and bridge global gaps. He specializes in creating impactful tools that address real-world challenges, with a particular focus on agriculture and global farmer networking. Driven by the belief that “The past is an illusion to my strength. I am not defined by what lies behind me but the courage and all the challenges I have been through,” Eusebious combines technical expertise with a deep commitment to innovation and social impact. His mission is to harness technology to create fair, transparent, and accessible platforms that benefit both local and advanced farmers worldwide.
